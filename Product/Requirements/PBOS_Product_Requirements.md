# PBOS Product Requirements

## Product Vision

PBOS is an AI-powered Business Operating System that helps established business owners continuously transform their Human Signature into Business Return and Life Return through authentic promotional video podcasting according to their Vision of Life.

PBOS is not podcast software.

PBOS is not an AI chatbot.

PBOS is a continuous business improvement operating system.

---

# Product Mission

Enable established business owners to build businesses that:

- create sustainable Business Return
- create meaningful Life Return
- protect Human Time
- leverage AI responsibly
- reduce burnout
- continuously improve through measurable recommendations

---

# Product Philosophy

PBOS exists to answer one question:

> What is the highest Business Return and Life Return I can create with my next unit of Human Time according to my Vision of Life?

Every feature should improve PBOS's ability to answer this question.

# Core User Journey

1. Connect business systems.
2. Assess the current business.
3. Calculate PBHS.
4. Identify bottlenecks.
5. Receive prioritized recommendations.
6. Decide DIY, DWY or DFY.
7. Execute with AI Employees.
8. Measure improvement.
9. Learn.
10. Repeat.

# Design Principles

Every feature should:

- protect Human Signature
- support the owner's Vision of Life
- increase Business Return
- increase Life Return
- create reusable assets
- reduce unnecessary work
- increase explainability
- preserve human accountability

---

# Non-Goals

PBOS does not aim to:

- maximize screen time
- replace authentic human expertise
- optimize vanity metrics
- encourage unnecessary content production
- maximize work hours
