# PBHS Capability Model

## Purpose

The PBHS Capability Model defines the canonical business capabilities measured by the Podcast Business Health System.

Capabilities represent stable characteristics of a business that influence its ability to create sustainable Business Return and Life Return.

Questions, evidence, scores and recommendations are all derived from these capabilities.

The Capability Model serves as the common language shared across PBOS.

---

# What is a Capability?

A capability is the demonstrated ability of a business to consistently perform an activity that creates value.

Capabilities are:

- measurable
- improvable
- explainable
- actionable
- reusable

Capabilities are not goals.

Capabilities create the conditions that make goals achievable.

# Initial PBHS Capability Set

Version 1 includes eight core capabilities.

---

## Human Signature

The owner's unique expertise, authenticity and judgment.

Purpose:

Create differentiation that cannot easily be copied or automated.

Executive Owner:

CEO

---

## Knowledge Assets

Reusable intellectual property created from Human Knowledge.

Purpose:

Generate value beyond direct service delivery.

Executive Owner:

Chief Knowledge Officer

---

## Podcast Assets

Strategic promotional content supporting business growth.

Purpose:

Increase visibility and trust.

Executive Owner:

Chief Content Officer

---

## Trust

The confidence customers place in the business and its expertise.

Purpose:

Reduce buying friction.

Executive Owner:

Chief Marketing Officer

---

## Business Systems

Documented and repeatable operational processes.

Purpose:

Increase scalability while reducing founder dependency.

Executive Owner:

Chief Operations Officer

---

## AI Leverage

Effective use of AI to amplify Human Signature.

Purpose:

Increase productivity while protecting Human Time.

Executive Owner:

Chief AI Officer

---

## Business Return

Long-term value created for the business.

Purpose:

Support sustainable growth.

Executive Owner:

Chief Financial Officer

---

## Life Return

Long-term value created for the business owner's life.

Purpose:

Support the owner's Vision of Life.

Executive Owner:

Chief Wellbeing Officer

# Capability Maturity

Every capability progresses through five maturity levels.

| Level | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Initial |
| 2 | Developing |
| 3 | Managed |
| 4 | Optimized |
| 5 | Transformational |

Capability maturity is independent of business size.

# Capability Improvement Cycle

Every capability follows the same improvement cycle.

```text
Measure

↓

Interpret

↓

Prioritize

↓

Improve

↓

Measure Again
```

Continuous improvement is preferred over one-time optimization.

# Design Principles

PBHS capabilities should be:

- observable
- measurable
- actionable
- coachable
- evidence-based
- stable over time
- independent but interconnected
