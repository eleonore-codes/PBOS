# Business

## Purpose

This folder contains the business architecture of CreatingReorganized.

While the **Architecture** folder defines how PBOS is designed and operates, the **Business** folder defines how CreatingReorganized creates, delivers and captures value through PBOS and future products.

Business architecture is the bridge between the company's mission and its implementation.

---

# Vision

CreatingReorganized empowers established business owners to transform their expertise into sustainable Business Return and Life Return through AI-assisted business systems centered around authentic promotional video podcasting.

---

# Mission

Design products, services and business models that enable business owners to:

- protect their Human Signature
- maximize Business Return
- maximize Life Return
- reduce burnout
- build reusable business assets
- create sustainable long-term growth according to their Vision of Life

---

# Scope

The Business folder answers questions such as:

- Who are our customers?
- What problems do we solve?
- Which products do we offer?
- How do we create value?
- How do we generate revenue?
- How do we grow sustainably?
- How do PBOS and future products fit together?

---

# Business Philosophy

CreatingReorganized believes that:

- Business should support life.
- Human expertise is the primary source of value.
- AI should amplify people rather than replace them.
- Sustainable systems outperform heroic effort.
- Time is the scarcest business resource.
- Trust is the primary mechanism through which promotional video podcasts create business value.

---

# Business Model

CreatingReorganized serves customers through three implementation paths:

## DIY

Customers build PBOS independently using software, documentation and self-paced learning.

---

## DWY

Customers implement PBOS through workshops, coaching and collaborative implementation.

---

## DFY

CreatingReorganized and its partners implement PBOS for the customer.
