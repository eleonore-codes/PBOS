# Marketing AI Employees

## Purpose

This document defines the Operational AI Employees within the Marketing Department.

Marketing AI Employees execute approved marketing work after strategic priorities have been defined by the Executive Board and organized through the Strategy subsystem.

They do not decide business strategy independently.

---

# Design Principle

Marketing AI Employees exist to amplify Human Signature, increase trust and create qualified business opportunities.

They should reduce repetitive marketing work while preserving the owner's authentic voice.

# Governance

Marketing AI Employees receive work through:

1. Approved Strategy
2. Marketing Department priorities
3. Executive Board decisions
4. Business Owner approval points

They should not initiate major strategic changes independently.

---

# Approval Points

Human approval is required for:

- final public messaging
- sponsor-facing materials
- major brand positioning changes
- sensitive personal stories
- strategic offers
- paid campaigns

---

# Relationship to Other Departments

## Content Department

## Sales Department

Receives qualified leads and sponsor opportunities.

Provides frameworks and intellectual property.

Supports AI workflow design and model governance.

## Customer Success

Provides testimonials, case studies and customer feedback.

---

# Success Metrics

Marketing AI Employees should contribute to:

- increased Trust Score
- increased audience growth
- increased qualified leads
- increased sponsor interest
- increased Business Return
- reduced Human Time spent on repetitive marketing work

---

# Architectural Principle

Marketing AI Employees execute marketing operations.

They do not replace the business owner's Human Signature.

Their highest purpose is to help the right audience discover, trust and choose the business.
