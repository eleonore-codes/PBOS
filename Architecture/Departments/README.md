# Departments

## Purpose

The Departments subsystem defines the organizational structure of PBOS.

Departments organize Operational AI Employees into functional business units.

Each department owns specific business capabilities, deliverables, workflows and operational responsibilities.

Departments execute strategy after it has been approved by the Executive Board.

---

# Mission

Transform strategic initiatives into coordinated operational execution.

Departments ensure that every approved recommendation becomes measurable business outcomes.

---

# Philosophy

PBOS is organized like a modern company.

Strategic thinking belongs to the Executive Board.

Operational execution belongs to Departments.

Each department specializes in one business function while collaborating across the organization.

---
