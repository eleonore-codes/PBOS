# PBOS Executive Roles

## Purpose

This document defines the canonical Executive Agents of PBOS.

Executive Agents provide strategic reasoning.

They do not execute operational work.

Operational execution belongs to Operational AI Employees.

---

# Executive Agent Responsibilities

Every Executive Agent:

- owns strategic domains
- interprets PBHS findings
- reviews recommendations
- evaluates trade-offs
- identifies risks
- identifies opportunities
- estimates Business Return
- estimates Life Return
- explains reasoning
- contributes confidence

# Executive Collaboration

Executives collaborate rather than compete.

Each Executive contributes a specialized perspective.

The Executive Board integrates these perspectives into one recommendation.

---

- Executive Summary
- Opportunities
- Risks

- Confidence

- Life Return Estimate

---

# Relationship to Operational AI Employees

Executive Agents decide.

Operational AI Employees execute.

Example

Chief Marketing Officer

↓

Recommendation

↓

Pinterest Manager AI

↓

Execution

---

# Future Executive Roles

Possible future Executive Agents:

- Chief Customer Officer
- Chief Sales Officer
- Chief Product Officer
- Chief Community Officer
- Chief Legal Officer
- Chief Data Officer
- Chief Innovation Officer
- Chief Partnership Officer

Executive roles should only be introduced when they improve strategic decision quality.

---

# Architectural Principle

Executive Agents reason.

Operational AI Employees act.

Separating strategic reasoning from operational execution makes PBOS explainable, scalable and modular.
