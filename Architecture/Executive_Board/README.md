# Executive Board

## Purpose

The Executive Board is the strategic reasoning layer of PBOS.

It simulates a multidisciplinary executive leadership team that reviews business evidence, evaluates opportunities and constraints, and contributes specialized perspectives before recommendations are presented to the business owner.

PBOS does not rely on a single AI opinion.

Instead, recommendations are strengthened through structured executive deliberation.

---

# Mission

The Executive Board exists to improve strategic decision quality.

Every major recommendation should benefit from multiple executive viewpoints before implementation.

---

# Philosophy

Different executives evaluate different aspects of a business.

Examples include:

- financial sustainability
- marketing impact
- operational feasibility
- AI leverage
- wellbeing
- long-term strategy

Combining these perspectives produces more balanced recommendations than relying on a single optimization objective.

---

# Core Responsibilities

The Executive Board:

- reviews PBHS findings
- evaluates recommendation quality
- identifies risks
- resolves trade-offs
- prioritizes investments
- protects Human Signature
- safeguards the owner's Vision of Life

---

# Executive Members

Initial Executive Board members include:

- CEO
- Chief Strategy Officer
- Chief Financial Officer
- Chief Marketing Officer
- Chief Content Officer
- Chief Operations Officer
- Chief AI Officer
- Chief Knowledge Officer
- Chief Wellbeing Officer

Additional executives may be introduced as PBOS evolves.

---

# Decision Flow

```text
PBHS
    ↓
Recommendation Engine
    ↓
Executive Board Review
    ↓
Business Owner
    ↓
Strategy
    ↓
Departments
```

---

# Design Principles

The Executive Board should be:

- evidence-based
- explainable
- transparent
- collaborative
- human-centered
- strategically aligned

---

# Relationship to PBOS

PBHS answers:

Where are we?

Recommendation Engine answers:

What should we do?

Executive Board answers:

Why is this the right decision?
