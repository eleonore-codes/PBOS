# Decision Framework

## Purpose

The Decision Framework is the strategic reasoning framework of PBOS.

It provides the common language, principles and evaluation criteria used by every Executive Agent when making recommendations.

The Decision Framework ensures that PBOS makes decisions consistently, transparently and in alignment with the business owner's Vision of Life.

---

# Mission

The Decision Framework exists to improve executive decision quality.

Rather than relying on intuition, isolated metrics or opaque AI reasoning, PBOS evaluates every important decision using a consistent set of evidence-based criteria.

---

# Philosophy

Every business decision is an investment.

An investment may consume:

- Human Time
- Money
- Attention
- Energy
- AI Capacity
- Business Assets

Every investment should create value.

PBOS evaluates value using both:

- Business Return
- Life Return

while protecting Human Signature.

---

# Core Questions

Every major decision should answer:

1. What problem are we solving?
2. Why is this the highest priority?
3. Which evidence supports this?
4. What Business Return is expected?
5. What Life Return is expected?
6. What are the risks?
7. What assumptions exist?
8. What should happen next?

---

# Decision Inputs

The Decision Framework receives:

- PBHS findings
- Recommendation Engine proposals
- Executive Board opinions
- Vision of Life
- Business constraints
- Human Time constraints
- Confidence estimates

---

# Decision Outputs

The framework produces:

- Recommendation Priority
- Investment Quality
- Business Return Estimate
- Life Return Estimate
- Risk Assessment
- Strategic Alignment
- Confidence
- Execution Recommendation

---

# Design Principles

The framework should always be:

- evidence-based
- explainable
- repeatable
- human-centered
- transparent
- continuously improving
