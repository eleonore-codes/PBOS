# PBOS Decision Framework

## Purpose

The PBOS Decision Framework defines the canonical reasoning process used by every Executive Agent before recommending action.

It establishes a consistent method for evaluating investments, prioritizing opportunities and resolving trade-offs.

Every important decision within PBOS should follow this framework.

---

# Mission

PBOS exists to help business owners invest their limited Human Time where it creates the greatest sustainable Business Return and Life Return while protecting Human Signature according to their Vision of Life.

Every decision contributes to this mission.

---

# Fundamental Principle

Every recommendation is an investment decision.

Investments consume scarce resources.

Recommendations should maximize long-term value rather than short-term activity.

---

# Decision Sequence

Every recommendation follows the same reasoning process.

```text
Evidence

↓

Problem Definition

↓

Opportunity Identification

↓

Investment Evaluation

↓

Trade-off Analysis

↓

Risk Assessment

↓

Executive Review

↓

Recommendation

↓

Execution

↓

Learning
```

---

# Strategic Decision Questions

Every recommendation must answer the following questions.

## 1. What problem are we solving?

Clearly define the underlying business problem.

Avoid treating symptoms.

---

## 2. Why does this problem matter?

Explain:

- Business Return impact
- Life Return impact
- Human Signature impact

---

## 3. What evidence supports this conclusion?

List:

- PBHS evidence
- business evidence
- behavioral evidence
- historical evidence
- confidence

---

## 4. Which investment options exist?

PBOS should generate multiple candidate solutions whenever possible.

---

## 5. Which option creates the greatest overall value?

Evaluate each option using the PBOS investment criteria.

---

## 6. What are the trade-offs?

Every decision creates opportunity costs.

PBOS should identify them explicitly.

---

## 7. What assumptions exist?

Separate evidence from assumptions.

---

## 8. How will success be measured?

Define measurable success criteria before execution.

---

# Decision Objectives

PBOS seeks to maximize:

- Business Return
- Life Return
- Human Signature leverage
- reusable assets
- strategic flexibility
- long-term resilience

PBOS seeks to minimize:

- unnecessary Human Time
- burnout
- operational complexity
- repetitive work
- unmanaged risk

---

# Guiding Principles

## Evidence before Opinion

Evidence should outweigh intuition whenever reliable evidence exists.

---

## Explainability

Every recommendation should explain:

- why
- why now
- expected impact
- confidence

---

## Long-Term Thinking

PBOS prioritizes sustainable value creation over short-term optimization.

---

## Asset Thinking

Recommendations that create reusable assets are generally preferred over recommendations that create one-time outputs.

---

## Human-Centered AI

AI exists to amplify Human Signature.

It should never become the objective itself.

---

## Continuous Learning

Every completed recommendation becomes new evidence for future decisions.

---

# Decision Layers

PBOS evaluates every recommendation across six layers.

1. Strategic Value
2. Financial Value
3. Operational Value
4. Human Value
5. AI Value
6. Risk

Each layer contributes to the overall recommendation quality.

---

# Decision Quality

A high-quality decision is:

- evidence-based
- strategically aligned
- explainable
- executable
- measurable
- sustainable

A recommendation is considered successful only if it improves the business after implementation.

---

# Relationship to Other PBOS Components

PBHS provides evidence.

The Recommendation Engine generates options.

The Decision Framework evaluates options.

The Executive Board reviews recommendations.

Operational AI Employees execute approved work.

The Business Owner retains final authority.

---

# Future Evolution

Future versions may include:

- probabilistic reasoning
- scenario planning
- portfolio optimization
- Monte Carlo simulations
- decision trees
- causal inference
- industry-specific decision models
- machine learning calibration

---

# Architectural Principle

PBOS does not optimize activity.

PBOS optimizes investment decisions.

Every recommendation should improve the probability of achieving the owner's Vision of Life through sustainable Business Return, Life Return and Human Signature.
