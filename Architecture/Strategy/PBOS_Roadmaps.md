# PBOS Roadmaps

## Purpose

PBOS Roadmaps organize strategic initiatives across time.

A roadmap answers:

> What should be executed first, next and later?

Roadmaps ensure that initiatives are sequenced according to strategic value, dependencies, available capacity and the owner's Vision of Life.

---

# Mission

Transform strategy into an executable timeline while maintaining flexibility as new evidence becomes available.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

PBOS does not produce rigid long-term plans.

Instead, it maintains adaptive roadmaps that evolve as PBHS assessments, Executive Board decisions and business conditions change.

# Roadmap Structure

Every roadmap contains:

- Time Horizon
- Strategic Objectives
- Active Initiatives
- Planned Projects
- Dependencies
- Resource Allocation
- Milestones
- Review Dates

---

# Roadmap Prioritization

Initiatives are ordered according to:

- Strategic Alignment
- Business Return
- Life Return
- Human Signature
- Human Time
- Dependencies
- Executive Priority
- Available Capacity

---

# Milestones

Each roadmap defines measurable milestones.

Examples:

- Sponsor Media Kit Published
- First Sponsor Acquired
- AI Employee Deployed
- Knowledge Framework Released
- Workflow Automated

Milestones represent completed outcomes rather than completed activities.

---

# Capacity Planning

PBOS plans work according to available capacity.

Capacity includes:

- Human Time
- Budget
- AI Capacity
- External Partners
- Operational Constraints

Roadmaps should never exceed available capacity.

---

# Dynamic Replanning

Roadmaps are continuously updated when:

- PBHS assessments change
- objectives are achieved
- new bottlenecks appear
- risks increase
- opportunities emerge
- Business Owner priorities change

---

# Governance

The Executive Board:

- approves roadmaps
- reviews progress
- reprioritizes initiatives
- reallocates resources

The Business Owner approves major roadmap changes.

---

# Success Indicators

A roadmap is successful when it:

- delivers strategic objectives
- improves Business Return
- improves Life Return
- protects Human Signature
- maintains strategic focus
- avoids unnecessary overload

---

# Relationship to Operational AI Employees

Roadmaps assign priorities.

Operational AI Employees execute the projects and deliverables associated with the current roadmap.

---

# Architectural Principle

Roadmaps organize time.

Initiatives organize investments.

Projects organize execution.

Together they transform strategy into sustainable progress.
