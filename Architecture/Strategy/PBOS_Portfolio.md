# PBOS Portfolio Model

## Purpose

The PBOS Portfolio Model defines how PBOS manages all strategic investments as a balanced portfolio rather than as independent projects.

The objective is to maximize long-term Business Return, Life Return and Human Signature while balancing available resources, strategic priorities and execution capacity.

---

# Mission

Enable the Business Owner and Executive Board to allocate limited resources toward the portfolio of initiatives that creates the greatest overall value.

PBOS manages an investment portfolio—not a task list.

---

# Portfolio Philosophy

Every recommendation becomes part of an investment portfolio.

The value of the portfolio is greater than the sum of its individual initiatives because initiatives reinforce one another.

PBOS therefore optimizes the portfolio as a whole rather than maximizing individual projects.

# Portfolio Evaluation

Every portfolio is evaluated using the PBOS Decision Criteria.

Evaluation includes:

- Business Return
- Life Return
- Human Signature
- Human Time
- Strategic Alignment
- Asset Creation
- AI Leverage
- Risk
- Confidence
- Learning Value

---

# Portfolio Balancing

PBOS seeks a balanced investment portfolio.

A balanced portfolio avoids over-investment in a single area.

Examples

Too much:

- content creation

Too little:

- sales
- systems
- AI
- wellbeing

The Executive Board periodically rebalances the portfolio.

---

# Resource Allocation

Resources include:

- Human Time
- Budget
- AI Capacity
- Team Capacity
- External Partners
- Existing Business Assets

Portfolio allocation should remain within available capacity.

---

# Executive Governance

The Executive Board is responsible for:

- approving portfolios
- allocating resources
- balancing investments
- retiring initiatives
- introducing new investments
- monitoring portfolio health

The Business Owner approves major strategic changes.

# Architectural Principle

PBOS manages portfolios of strategic investments rather than isolated projects.

Every investment should contribute to a balanced portfolio that advances the owner's Vision of Life while increasing Business Return, Life Return and Human Signature.
