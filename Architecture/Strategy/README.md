# Strategy

## Purpose

The Strategy subsystem translates executive decisions into coherent business direction.

While the Executive Board determines **what the best investments are**, the Strategy subsystem organizes those investments into objectives, initiatives, roadmaps and portfolios.

Its purpose is to ensure that PBOS executes a coordinated strategy rather than a disconnected collection of tasks.

---

# Mission

The Strategy subsystem exists to transform recommendations into a focused strategic plan that maximizes Business Return and Life Return according to the owner's Vision of Life.

---

# Philosophy

PBOS does not execute recommendations independently.

Recommendations are grouped into strategic initiatives.

Initiatives support strategic objectives.

Objectives support the owner's Vision of Life.

Every operational activity should contribute to a clearly defined strategic objective.

---

# Core Questions

The Strategy subsystem answers:

- What are we trying to achieve?
- Why is this strategically important?
- Which initiatives should we prioritize?
- Which recommendations belong together?
- What should we postpone?
- What should we stop doing?
- How will we measure success?

- Quarterly Roadmap

---

# Guiding Principles

Every strategy should:

- align with the Vision of Life
- maximize Business Return
- maximize Life Return
- protect Human Signature
- create reusable assets
- reduce unnecessary complexity
- remain explainable
- adapt as new evidence becomes available
