# Architecture

## Purpose

This folder contains the canonical software, systems and knowledge architecture of PBOS.

The architecture defines the concepts, principles, relationships and decision models that govern how PBOS operates.

The architecture is designed to remain stable over many software versions and serves as the single source of truth for developers, AI agents and business stakeholders.

---

# Scope

The Architecture folder answers questions such as:

- What is PBOS?
- What are the core business concepts?
- How do those concepts relate?
- How does PBOS create value?
- How does PBOS make recommendations?
- How does PBOS continuously improve a business?
- How should future features integrate with the existing architecture?

---

# Architectural Principles

The PBOS architecture follows several guiding principles.

## Human before AI

Artificial Intelligence amplifies Human Signature.

It does not replace it.

---

## Business before Podcast

The podcast is a strategic business asset.

It is not the business itself.

---

## Assets before Activities

PBOS prioritizes reusable business assets over isolated tasks.

---

## Compounding before Linear Growth

Knowledge Assets and Podcast Assets should continue creating value long after they are produced.

---

## Sustainable before Maximum

Business Return should always support Life Return according to the owner's Vision of Life.
