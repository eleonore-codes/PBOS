# Life Return (Value Object)

## Purpose

Life Return represents the positive contribution a business decision, investment or activity makes toward the Business Owner's desired way of living.

It complements Business Return by ensuring that business success is evaluated not only financially, but also by its impact on the owner's quality of life.

PBOS optimizes Business Return and Life Return together.

---

# Definition

Life Return is a Value Object.

It has no independent identity.

Its value is derived from measurable improvements in the Business Owner's life.

---

# Mission

Provide a common framework for evaluating whether business decisions support the owner's Vision of Life.

---

# Characteristics

Life Return is:

- measurable
- explainable
- evidence-based
- personal
- long-term
- continuously reviewed

Possible indicators include:

- Hours recovered
- Stress reduction

- Vacation days protected
- Founder dependency reduced
- Wellbeing score

---

# Time Horizons

Life Return may be evaluated over:

- Immediate
- Short-Term
- Quarterly
- Annual
- Long-Term

Long-term Life Return should generally receive greater weight than short-term convenience.

---

# Design Principles

Life Return should:

- align with the Vision of Life
- remain measurable
- balance business ambitions
- encourage sustainability
- avoid burnout
- preserve Human Signature

---

# Example

Recommendation

Automate podcast publishing.

Expected Life Return

- Save four hours each week.
- Reduce repetitive administrative work.
- Increase uninterrupted family time.
- Lower cognitive load.

Measured after six months

- Four hours saved per week.
- Thirty percent reduction in administrative effort.
- Family evenings consistently protected.

---

# Relationship to Business Return

PBOS evaluates Business Return and Life Return together.

A recommendation with exceptional Business Return but unacceptable Life Return should normally receive lower priority unless explicitly approved by the Business Owner.

---

# Architectural Principle

Life Return represents the value created for the Business Owner's life.

The purpose of PBOS is not simply to build a successful business, but to build a business that supports a successful life.
